How Credits are used

Pay for successful story actions, not AI requests

20 CreditsCreate a new active-action Story Run
1 CreditPublish a suggested action by a human player
2 CreditsPublish a custom or complex action by a human player
0 CreditsReading, retries, failures, and AI-controlled actions

A Credit reservation is confirmed only when the story result is successfully published. A final generation failure releases it. A host sponsorship is a 10-Credit allowance for one player in one Run; it does not increase that player’s personal wallet. Worlds still using the legacy unlock policy show that policy before room creation and do not use the 20-Credit active-action creation fee.
