# Legal Pages Deployment Checklist

Use this checklist before publishing the Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Refund Policy.

## Required replacements

Replace every occurrence of:

- `[LEGAL ENTITY NAME]`
- `[REGISTERED BUSINESS ADDRESS]`
- `[GOVERNING LAW/JURISDICTION]`
- `[COURT LOCATION/JURISDICTION]`

Do not publish unresolved placeholders.

## Recommended routes

- `/privacy` — Privacy Policy
- `/terms` — Terms of Service
- `/refund` — Refund Policy

Add permanent footer links to all three pages.

## Signup and checkout

- Require users to confirm that they are at least 16.
- Add a signup statement linking to the Terms and Privacy Policy.
- Record the policy version and acceptance timestamp.
- Before payment, link to the Refund Policy.
- For EEA/UK immediate digital delivery, collect any required express consent and acknowledgement concerning immediate performance and withdrawal rights.

## Privacy implementation

- Provide an account-deletion control or process requests sent to `support@ourmanyworlds.com`.
- Make the stated 30-day deletion target operational.
- Ensure backups are removed or overwritten within the stated 90-day period.
- Configure AI API providers not to use submitted API data for general model training where available.
- Sign appropriate data-processing agreements with vendors.
- Maintain a current internal list of subprocessors and data locations.
- Use a consent banner before non-essential analytics or marketing cookies where required.
- Do not add targeted advertising or sell/share personal data without updating the policy and implementing required opt-outs.

## Product and moderation

- Clearly label AI-generated content in the interface.
- Keep moderation and reporting channels available.
- Do not claim that AI output is factual or professional advice.
- Ensure multiplayer UI clearly distinguishes private character information from room-visible information.

## Payments and refunds

- Confirm that Creem checkout and receipts show the correct merchant and support contact.
- Test full and partial refund workflows.
- Log refund requests, decisions, transaction IDs, and processing dates.
- Ensure used credits can be identified before refund approval.

## Review cadence

Review all legal pages whenever you change:

- the legal entity or business address;
- minimum user age;
- payment provider;
- AI model provider or training settings;
- analytics, cookies, or advertising;
- data retention periods;
- pricing, credits, subscriptions, or refund rules;
- countries actively targeted by the Service.

A qualified lawyer should review these documents before a major commercial launch or substantial international scale-up.
