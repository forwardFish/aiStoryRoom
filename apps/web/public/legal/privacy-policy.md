## 6. AI Processing and Generated Content

The Service uses artificial intelligence to create and transform stories, character behavior, summaries, and related content.

To provide these features:

- your prompts, actions, game context, and relevant room content may be sent to contracted AI model providers;
- AI output may be generated automatically and may be inaccurate, incomplete, repetitive, unexpected, or offensive;
- automated systems may assist with content moderation, fraud detection, abuse prevention, and product safety;
- we do not use automated processing to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you.

We do not intentionally use private gameplay content to train a model made generally available to other customers. Where technically and contractually available, we configure AI service providers not to use API-submitted content for general model training.

AI-generated content should be treated as fictional entertainment and not as professional, legal, medical, financial, psychological, or safety advice.

## 7. Multiplayer Rooms and Content Visibility

Our Many Worlds is a multiplayer service. Depending on the room design and your choices:

- your display name, avatar, role, actions, messages, choices, and generated story content may be visible to other room participants;
- some information may be private to your character, while other information may become public to the room as part of gameplay;
- room creators or hosts may control invitations, roles, and certain room settings;
- other participants may copy, save, or share content they can access.

Review the room settings and avoid sharing personal information that you do not want other participants to see.
