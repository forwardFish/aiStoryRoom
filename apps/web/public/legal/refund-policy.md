# Refund Policy

**Effective date:** July 12, 2026
**Last updated:** July 12, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to purchases of credits, world access, story extensions, timeline resets, and other digital products offered through **Our Many Worlds** at **ourmanyworlds.com**.

Our Many Worlds is operated by **[LEGAL ENTITY NAME]** (**“we,” “us,” or “our”**).

Nothing in this Policy limits any mandatory consumer right that cannot lawfully be waived.

## 1. Payment Processing and Merchant of Record

Purchases may be processed by **Creem** or another third-party merchant of record or payment provider.

Where Creem acts as merchant of record:

- Creem is the seller shown for the checkout transaction and may appear on the receipt or payment statement;
- the purchase may also be subject to Creem's buyer terms;
- approved refunds are processed through Creem and the original payment method where possible;
- Creem may issue refunds where required by law or its applicable terms.

For product-specific issues, contact us first at **support@ourmanyworlds.com**.

## 2. Standard Refund Window

You may request a refund within **14 calendar days** after purchase if:

- the purchased credits or digital access have not been used, consumed, transferred, or materially delivered;
- the request is made by the purchaser using the purchase email address;
- the purchase was not made through fraud, abuse, or a prohibited payment method.

Approved eligible requests will normally receive a refund to the original payment method.

## 3. Refunds for Technical or Billing Problems

We will provide a refund, replacement access, or account credit, as appropriate, when:

- you were charged more than once for the same purchase;
- payment succeeded but the purchased credits or access were not delivered;
- a material technical defect prevented you from using the purchased digital product and we could not provide a reasonable fix or replacement;
- the charge was unauthorized and the claim is verified;
- a refund is required by applicable law.

Before issuing a refund for a technical problem, we may ask for reasonable information such as the transaction ID, device or browser details, screenshots, and error messages.

## 4. Items Generally Not Eligible for Refund

Except where required by law, refunds are generally not available for:

- credits that have already been spent or used to generate, unlock, extend, or reset content;
- a story, character, result, AI output, or ending that you personally disliked;
- content that was successfully generated but differed from your expectations;
- accidental use of credits after they were correctly delivered;
- suspension or termination resulting from a material violation of our Terms of Service;
- bonus, promotional, complimentary, or free credits;
- currency-conversion differences, bank fees, or payment-provider fees outside our control;
- requests made more than 14 days after purchase, unless the product was defective or applicable law requires otherwise.

AI-generated storytelling is variable by nature. Dissatisfaction with narrative style or outcome alone does not normally make a successfully delivered digital service defective.

## 5. Partially Used Purchases

If only part of a credit package has been used, we may, at our discretion or where required by law:

- deny the refund;
- provide a proportional refund for the unused paid portion; or
- provide replacement credits or access.

Promotional or bonus credits are excluded when calculating any proportional refund.

## 6. European Economic Area and United Kingdom Consumers

Consumers in the EEA or UK may have a statutory right to withdraw from certain distance contracts within 14 days.

For digital content or services supplied immediately, checkout may ask you to:

1. expressly request immediate performance or delivery; and
2. acknowledge that your withdrawal right may be reduced or lost after digital content is supplied or performance begins, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

If the required consent or acknowledgement was not properly obtained, your statutory rights remain unaffected.

Digital content or services that are faulty, not as described, or not supplied remain subject to applicable consumer remedies regardless of the standard refund window.

## 7. How to Request a Refund

Email **support@ourmanyworlds.com** with the subject line **“Refund Request.”**

Include:

- the email address used for the purchase;
- the transaction or order ID;
- the purchase date and amount;
- the product or credit package purchased;
- a brief explanation of the request;
- screenshots or error details, if the request concerns a technical problem.

Do not send full payment-card numbers, passwords, or card security codes.

## 8. Review and Processing Time

We aim to:

- acknowledge refund requests within **2 business days**;
- make an eligibility decision within **5 business days** after receiving the information reasonably needed to review the request;
- submit an approved refund promptly through the merchant of record or payment provider.

After a refund is submitted, the payment provider or bank may take approximately **5 to 10 business days**, and sometimes longer, to post the funds. Actual timing depends on the payment method, provider, bank, currency, and country.

## 9. Refund Effects

When a refund is approved:

- the related credits or digital access may be removed from your account;
- content or features unlocked solely by that purchase may become unavailable;
- any negative credit balance caused by prior use must be resolved before further purchases or use;
- associated promotional credits may also be removed.

## 10. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

Contact us before initiating a chargeback so that we can investigate and attempt to resolve the issue.

Fraudulent or abusive chargebacks may result in temporary account restrictions while the dispute is reviewed. This does not prevent you from exercising lawful rights with your card issuer or payment provider.

## 11. Purchases Through Other Platforms

If you purchase through an app store, marketplace, or platform that controls refunds, you must request the refund through that platform. Its refund policy and procedures may apply.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy for future purchases. The version shown at the time of purchase will generally apply to that transaction, unless a later version gives you greater rights or applicable law requires otherwise.
